The Island of Time

Seven years after unleashing the Sands of Time, the Prince is hunted by the Dahaka — an unstoppable guardian of the Timeline. His only escape lies on the mythical Island of Time.

Armed with dual weapons and fueled by vengeance, he must fight through sand creatures, navigate deadly traps, and confront the Empress of Time herself.
